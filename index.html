How it works

Running in under a minute.

Paste a link. Connect your channels. Walk away. That's the whole setup.

01
Paste your business link
Your Google Business, Instagram, or website URL. Guest.Manager reads your hours, services, prices, and FAQs — no manual entry.
02
Connect your channels
Link WhatsApp, SMS, Instagram, or Facebook. All conversations land in one inbox — yours to monitor or hand off to the AI.
03
It handles the messages
Answers questions, confirms bookings, follows up. Flags what needs you — and stays quiet on everything else.
